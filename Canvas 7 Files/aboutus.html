About Us

'International Journal Corner' is a division of 'Globeedu Group'. This is a ISO 9001:2015 certified organization based in India. 'International Journal Corner' is a single platform designed to disseminate research findings from various academic disciplines. All four journals under the IJC open-access platform aim at spreading latest experiments and cutting edge wisdom to a global audience, thereby bringing the world together for a greater efficiency and innovation. IJC is categorizing ever-increasing academic specialization into three principal segments with equal concentration on every single arena of education. But, the sure success consists only in the assimilation of all canons of study. Through this innovative effort, we have enabled the authors undertaking research on numerous different topics find their destination in a single portal, thereby putting an end to their chase to different publishers. Scholars, apprentices, trainees and students can choose their priority and go for the registration.
